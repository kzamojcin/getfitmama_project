Meet Fit Mama

Let's have a little talk about me ;)

Anna Godek - Get Fit Mama

I am highly qualified personal trainer who ensures that your training is effective and right for you.

Whatever your fitness level or goals I will help you to become fitter, healthier and reach your personal targets.

My training is a combination of Functional Training with Pilates and Yoga. Straightening and flexibility exercises will make you stronger but also prevent injuries so you can exercise safely during the pregnancy as well as get back into shape when the baby is born.

My focus is set on:

  • - Improving posture,
  • - Strengthening the core and also the whole body,
  • - Releasing pain and discomfort during pregnancy,
  • - Preparing for the birth,
  • - Helping with postpartum problems (urinary incontinence, diastasis recti, prolapse, back, neck and pelvic pain),
  • - Helping with menopausal symptoms,
  • - Weight loss.

I know how hard and frustrating it is to regain your vitality after pregnancy. I’ve been there myself.

After my second pregnancy, I found out that I had Diastasis Recti (abdominal separation), which caused a few back injuries and weakness in my core.

It was completely unexpected as I was so strong and fit before and during pregnancy. I had to start my training very slowly and used the right core improving exercises, which helped me to fully recover.

Now I am on a mission to help other women.

To prevent any postpartum complications, get strong and fit after the baby is born and further down the road, so they can enjoy motherhood and life.

That is why I designed my getfitmama exercise programs.

With 14 years experience in fitness, nutrition and manual therapy I can help pregnant and postpartum women as well as women after 40’s to get fit and healthy.
