Menopause

Maintaining bone strength, preserving cardiovascular health and flexibility and

good posture are just few of important exercise-related goals at this stage in life.

Hunched posture:

  • - Decreases your lungs and breathing capacity,
  • - Reduces overhead shoulder motion,
  • - Causes shoulder, neck, back, and rib pain

To prevent these complications, the appropriate upper body strength training and

stretches are crucial. As you go through menopause, regular exercise and a balanced diet can help with symptoms of menopause

Symptoms of menopause:

  • - Night sweats,
  • - Hot flushes,
  • - Irritability and moodiness,
  • - Weight gain

Urinary incontinence can also be an issue that can be lessened by effective core restoring exercises.

Menopause is an important transition in every woman’s life.

Use it as a reminder to take good care of yourself.
